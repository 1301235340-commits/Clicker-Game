# 🎮 Clicker Game - Enhanced Edition

A fast-paced, visually stunning idle clicker game with particle effects, combo multipliers, and progressive power-ups.

## 🎯 Features

- **🔥 Combo System**: Build momentum by clicking rapidly! Reach up to 2.5x multiplier at 50+ combo
- **✨ Particle Effects**: Dynamic particles and floating labels with each click
- **⚡ Power-Ups System**: Unlock power-ups at milestones to increase click value
- **📊 Persistent Stats**: Your progress is saved automatically in localStorage
- **🎨 Modern UI**: Dark theme with glowing effects and smooth animations
- **📱 Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop and mobile devices

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click the large red button to earn points
2. Rapid consecutive clicks build up your combo multiplier (max 2.5x at 50+ combo)
3. Combo resets if you stop clicking for more than 1.5 seconds
4. Unlock power-ups at click milestones to increase earnings per click
5. Watch your score grow with satisfying visual feedback!

## 🚀 Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in your browser to play!
